# Yawa AI Research Lab

> **Building AI Humans Can Trust.**

Welcome to **Yawa AI Research Lab**, an independent research initiative dedicated to advancing Trustworthy Artificial Intelligence through rigorous research, open science, and reproducible experimentation.

## Research Focus

- Trustworthy AI
- Large Language Models
- Explainable AI (XAI)
- Neuro-Symbolic AI
- Knowledge Graphs
- AI Safety
- Intelligent Systems

## Flagship Projects

- 🚀 TRUST-AI
- 📊 TrustBench
- 📈 TrustEval
- 🧠 TrustGraph
- 🔍 TrustExplain
- ⚖️ TrustReason

## Research Philosophy

We believe that future AI systems should not only be intelligent but also transparent, reliable, explainable, and worthy of human trust.

## Mission

To advance Artificial Intelligence through open, reproducible, and mathematically grounded research.
